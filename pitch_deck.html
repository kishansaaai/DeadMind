Key Differentiators

  • Relative Threshold Filtering: Our cross-encoder dynamically drops noise based on the query's best match, maintaining 80% P@3 while eliminating citation padding.
  • Real Consensus Engine: Weights engineer expertise based on retrieval confidence, evidence depth, and peer feedback.
  • Cognitive Fingerprints: AI responses align with the specific diagnostic style (Systematic vs. Intuitive) of the original engineer.
  • True Uncertainty Metric: Computes sparsity, staleness, and causal risk scores directly from the source distribution, instead of faking it.

Built for Production Reliability

Graceful Degradation

  • LLM Outages: Local grounded fallback builder synthesizes templated responses mimicking the target engineer.
  • STT Failures: Server-side Whisper transcription seamlessly falls back to client transcripts on unparsable audio.

  • Model Caching: Pre-warmed local embeddings and rerankers eliminate cold start issues.
